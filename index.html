How it works

From raw data to insights in minutes

No ETL pipelines. No BI setup. Just connect your Postgres database and go.

1

Connect your database

Provide read-only credentials to your Postgres, MySQL, or BigQuery database. We detect your schema automatically.

2

Map your tables

Tell us which tables hold customers, plans, and subscriptions. We do the rest with our battle-tested SQL templates.

3

Review your metrics

Your KPI dashboard is live instantly. MRR, churn, cohort charts — all refreshed on a schedule you control.

4

Share with your team

Invite teammates, export to CSV, or schedule email reports. Keep everyone on the same page with live data.

-- Monthly Recurring Revenue by plan SELECT DATE_TRUNC('month', s.start_date) AS month, p.plan_name, SUM(p.monthly_price) AS mrr FROM subscriptions s JOIN plans p USING (plan_id) WHERE s.end_date IS NULL OR s.end_date > NOW() GROUP BY 1, 2 ORDER BY 1 DESC; -- Monthly churn rate WITH churned AS ( SELECT DATE_TRUNC('month', end_date) AS month, COUNT(*) AS churned_customers FROM subscriptions WHERE end_date IS NOT NULL GROUP BY 1 ) SELECT month, churned_customers FROM churned;
