# Business Questions & Assumptions

## Core Questions
1. How much Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) does the company generate?
2. How fast is revenue growing month over month?
3. What percentage of customers churn each month?
4. Which customer cohorts retain the longest?

## Assumptions
- Customers subscribe to a single plan at a time
- Billing is monthly
- Churn occurs when a customer cancels their subscription
